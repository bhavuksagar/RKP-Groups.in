About Us

RKP Group started as an idea of Mr. Ajay Kumar in the year 2004. This was not possible without being a visionary and an Optimistic Leader, while creating a team of talented people. A single company was soon formed into a group of companies known as RKP Group, with young team of professionals in their pursuit. The founder continuously works with combined experience of several other experts in this field of 18 years and more. The team continuously works to establish and enterprise, sound business ethics and practices, progress through customer satisfaction and develop people who value relationship and professionalism. RKP Group is member of MSME (Ministry of Micro Small and Medium enterprises, Food security and FSSAI (Food Safety and Standards Act of India).

The Organization

Over the years RKP Group has grown as one of the reputed brands in FMCG Product. They have staff strength of more than 50 people covering 4 locations (Delhi, Haryana, Jharkhand, Bihar, M.P, Chhattisgarh, Uttrakhand and U.P.) and associates in other state. RKP Group understands that clients always have a specific requirement with regards to their shipments. We therefore spend considerable time to consult distributor / dealer/ retailer individually to understand their specific requirements and provide a customized solution. The loyal distributor / dealer/ retailer has helped us to grow at a rate of 30% year after year.
